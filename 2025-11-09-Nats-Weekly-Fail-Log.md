### Photo Assignment 2
I really enjoyed this assignment as it really allows you to see in different perspectives. I think that is so important especially when you are conceptualizing something in an image. All images tell a story as we learned in the apparatus theory videos. To take something and change the context of it is to change its story and our undeerstanding of it.
I chose to change the lighting, background, and angle in these images. The original image in assignment 1 had a black backdrop with three sources of lighting and a scale.

The first image above shows the wooden figurine on a black purse, which is hanging on the wall. This image has two sources of lighting. The lighting is a main light coming in from the top and a side light which is coming in from the right side of the image. I chose this background because I thought a purse hanging on a wall with a wooden figure was very different from what people normally see. It was also taller than me so I feel like the image is a bit imposing. 

The second image pictured above is an image of the wooden figurine on a desk next to a light. I chose this because this is the normal spot for this figurine. I thought it would be important to see the normal environment after seeing two other images, the first in assignment one and the second in this assignment, of the figure out of place. This image only has one source of light, which is the light next to it. Additionally, the angle is downwards.
